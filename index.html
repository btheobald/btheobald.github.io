GX80 Battery Grip

‹ ›

4000mAh+ Battery Grip for a mirrorless camera.

I tend to take a lot of photos when i'm hiking. While currently I use a battery pack to recharge while out and about, on longer trips I prefer to keep this for keeping my phone charged.

My solution is an integrated battery pack which connects to a custom battery adapter through the battery door. This design supports 2 18650, 20700 or 21700 cells, providing more than 4000mAh of capacity at a similar cost of a first party 1000mAh cell.

Used Autodesk Fusion360 for 3D printing models, KiCAD for circuit design and PCB layout.

Status: Casing Verification and Electronics Development.
