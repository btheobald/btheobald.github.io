About Me

‹ ›

I am studying for a Masters in Electrical and Electronic Engineering at the University of Southampton.

Now in my final year of study, I am looking for ways to expand my design and construction skills in industrial and commercial settings.

Electronics has been something of a hobby for several years prior to the beginning of my studies, mainly focusing on applying my programming and hardware design skills.

I have been programming in C and C++ for over 6 years, recently more aimed at embedded applications. Some of my projects are released publicly on GitHub.

My main goal when working on a project is to produce work of a professional quality, applying an eye of detail to physical, electronic and computational aspects of design. I hope that the projects shown here demonstrate that.

Currently I am looking for internship placements for the summer, starting from early June through to late September in line with the last year of my course.
