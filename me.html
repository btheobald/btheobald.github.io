About Me

‹ ›

I am studying MEng Electrical and Electronic Engineering at the University of Southampton.

Now in my second year of study, I am looking for ways to expand my skills in design and construction of electronics in industrial and commercial settings.

Electronics has been something of a hobby for several years prior to me starting to study, mainly focusing on applying my programming skills to AVR microcontrollers.

I have mainly been programming in C and C++ for over 6 years, and recently more aimed at embedded applications, I have some of my projects released publicly on GitHub.

My main goal when working on a project is always to produce work to a professional quality, applying an eye of detail to physical, electronic and computational aspects of design.
